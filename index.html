Important notice
This is not a medical diagnosis
This test is a preliminary risk assessment tool and is for informational purposes only. Please note:
  • The test does not replace a medical examination, laboratory diagnostics, or clinical assessment.
  • The test result is not a diagnosis, treatment prescription, or medical recommendation.
  • Hantavirus infection can only be diagnosed in a laboratory (PCR, IgM serology) and clinically by an infectious disease physician.
  • If you have acute shortness of breath, hemoptysis, loss of consciousness, or a sudden drop in blood pressure — call emergency services immediately (911 / 112), without waiting for the test result.
  • The authors of the test are not responsible for decisions made based on it.
If you are concerned about your condition — consult a doctor. Only a specialist can diagnose and treat you. Take care of yourself and your loved ones.

Hantavirus
infection risk test

Preliminary assessment of infection probability. Choose a test format below.

Important warning

This test is not a medical diagnosis. Hantavirus infection is a rare but extremely dangerous disease: mortality in pulmonary form reaches 35–40%. Early symptoms are almost indistinguishable from the flu. The test is designed for preliminary risk assessment and to help you decide whether to see a doctor.

Read each question carefully and select the answer that best describes your situation over the last 1–7 days (unless otherwise stated). At the end you will receive a total score and interpretation of the results.
